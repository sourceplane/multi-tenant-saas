## Objective

Extract the duplicated org-id resolution and idempotency-key read helpers
into a single new module `packages/cli/src/commands/helpers.ts` and have
both `writes.ts` and `webhook-secrets-rotate.ts` import from it. Pure
behaviour-preserving refactor: zero observable CLI behaviour change, zero
new commands, zero contract or SDK edits. The goal is to remove the
inline-duplication hazard before the next CLI write/rotate surface lands.
